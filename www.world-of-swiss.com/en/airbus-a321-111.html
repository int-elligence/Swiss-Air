    Efficiency beyond Europe

    The A321-111 flies short- and medium-haul routes with large numbers of passengers. It has a maximum of 200 seats and a cargo capacity of 4.3 tonnes. This airbus is used within Europe and on routes between Switzerland, Africa and the Middle East.

    HB-IOC Zermatt

    Named on 15 February 2011

    One in three tourists travels to Switzerland by air and a good number of them choose to fly with the national carrier. One particularly popular destination is Zermatt at the foot of the world-famous Matterhorn mountain in Valais. The christening of the airbus "Zermatt" was held here on the church square.
